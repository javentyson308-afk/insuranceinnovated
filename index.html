"If I die tomorrow — does my family keep the house? Or does the bank take it while they're still grieving?"

Your mortgage does not pause for grief. The moment you are gone or too ill to work, the lender's clock starts ticking. Mortgage Protection Insurance pays off your full loan balance — so your family does not lose their home on top of losing you.

  • ◆Coverage term-matched exactly to your loan length
  • ◆Family retains the home — no forced emergency sale
